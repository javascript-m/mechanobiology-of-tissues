Conference title: Mechanobiology of Tissues

Mechanobiology of tissues will be held at Max-Planck-Zentrum für Physik und Medizin from April 15th to 17th, we invite young investigators to submit an abstract to present their work.

Short summary: 

Mechanobiology explores how physical forces and mechanical properties influence biological systems. A conference on Mechanobiology Meets Physics brings together researchers from physics, biology, engineering, and quantitative sciences to understand how mechanical principles shape cellular and tissue behavior. 

Programm date: 15.04.2026 to 17.04.2026 
Location: MPI for Physics and Medicine 

Basic info for abstract submission:  There are 12 available slots for student talks, each consisting of a 15-minute presentation. If you would like to present your work as a talk, please submit your abstract using the following link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdC-BzqCK6JPmVC_ZrRES_g9Hp9WPT9YuYxZ89TpSSsh-IELg/viewform?usp=dialog . The deadline for abstract submission is March 23. Please submit the abstract in PDF form. 

In addition, there will be a poster session during the retreat, where students will have the opportunity to present their work and discuss their research.
